Hi, I'm Coley!

Here are my tips.

Confusion is a-okay!

The first time I'm introduced to something new, I let it go over my head. I used to stress out and try to retain all kinds of details that then wouldn't sink in until the third or fourth time I heard them. The stress didn't help and resulted in frustration. Don't let it! Sit back, absorb what you can, and then start practicing!

You're great exactly where you are.

We're all learning. Everyone starts as a beginner. When working with my back-end developers, I get overwhelmed by their depth of knowledge as compared to my inch-deep understanding. Then I remind myself that they've been doing this for a long time. It's all good! I'll keep learning and I'll get better! In the meantime, I'm doing great being where I am.
