Hi, my name is Ryan and I have some advice for you!

Don't try to learn too much at once!

Learning new things can be exciting. But don't try to blast through an entire course in one sitting. Give yourself an opportunity to practice a new idea before you move on to something new.

Take breaks. Learn in small chunks. Put things into practice on your own. Don't do too much in one day.

Find time to meditate!

This has nothing to do with coding. It's just good advice.

Give yourself 10 minutes every day to find some stillness. Your mind will thank you.
