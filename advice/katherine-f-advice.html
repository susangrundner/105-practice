Hi, my name is Katherine and here are somethings that have help me with learing to code!

Keep taking action!

There are times when I've felt completely overwhelmed, confused and have questioned whether I have the right brain type to even be a good coder. What I've found to be the most helpful when this happens is to recognize the thought/feeling and refocus on my commitment to take action.

This helps me focus on learning, which builds my skills and my confidence

A few other things that have helped me:

  1. taking notes
  2. reviewing my notes at the end of a study session
  3. taking breaks and moving my body
  4. reading code as much as I can
