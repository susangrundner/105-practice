Hi. I'm Casey & I'm great.
I have some advice for newbie coders.

You won't know it all.

You will never stop learning. There is always more to learn & there will always be someone who knows more than you do. Learn from them.

Know that you will fail.

It is part of the journey, and it is the best way to learn. Making mistakes shows that you are trying. Correcting them shows that you are learning.

Get creative.

Play around. Explore. Don't be afraid to experiment, to try new things.
You never know what you'll stumble upon.

Don't give up!

  • You will fail.
  • You will make mistakes.
  • You will get frustrated.
  • You feel lost.
  • You will think you cannot do it.

You can!
