Here's a couple thoughts for you:

You WILL feel discouraged...

And thats OK

When you start out you won't know how much you actually know untill you mess things up. Trust me, you'll mess things up but they CAN be fixed and you'll do it. You may need help but thats the really great thing about skillcrush, just reach out! Someone else has had the same problem and they'll point you in the right direction to fix it. Everything you learn, lesson to lesson, adds up fast and you'll look back in a little while and be blown away at what you know. If you have any questions, there's always google!!

You CAN help someone...

And you SHOULD

When you get to this point there will be generic 'advice' in this section. Take the time to actually write genuine advice. This advice page is supposed to be for newbies but trust me, I still feel like one and you probably will too. The reality is that you DO know more than you think you do. Take your knowledge and help someone else who may be having a hard time.
