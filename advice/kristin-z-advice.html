Hi, my name is Kristin and I have some advice for you!

Make Mistakes!

Making mistakes is a part of learning. Everyone says that, but it's because it's true! My biggest learning moments came from making mistakes and figuring out why things didn't work the way I thought they should.

Push Through!

It feels bad to suck at something, or to not "get it" as fast as you think you should. Keep pushing through that discomfort and it will eventually click for you. Keep trying, push through the frustration. You will get this!

Ask Yourself...

Pretend you're talking with someone who could help you, like a Skillcrush instructor or other student. Go through the process as if you would be explaining it to them. You might just figure the problem out yourself by talking it through out loud.
