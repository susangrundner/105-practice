Hi, my name is Sebrina and my advice for you is...

Ask questions!

There's a team of great Skillcrush instructors there for you when you need them! They're always very helpful, understanding & friendly! There is also a great Skillcrush community out there too, don't be shy to reach out for help.

The internet is your friend.

During your lessons in classes you get a slew of great resources to use & learn more from. Don't forget about these & go out to search for more too, there are endless awesome resources available to you out there on the interwebs.

Don't forget to take breaks when you need to.

Step away & breathe, if you need to come back to the project the following day, that is ok!
