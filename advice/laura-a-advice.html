Hi, my name is Laura and I have some advice for you!

Don't be afraid of breaking your code.

Always make a backup and save often. Then dig in and have fun!

Keep your code clean and simple.

Think about solving problems before writing your code. Think as much about deleting code as you think about writing code.

Remember that coding is a team effort.

Write helpful comments. And, always document your work.
