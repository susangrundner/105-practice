Hi, my name is Matt and I have some advice for you!

Learning tech skills can feel overwhelming at times so here's a few things to keep in mind!

Embrace the challenge and have patience - You are fully capable of doing this!

Approach it as a practice - This takes the pressure off of you to get it right straight away.

Practice often - This keeps your new skills fresh in your mind and minimises the fear of coming back to it.

Don't be hard on yourself - This is supposed to be fun!

Give yourself a high-five for taking on this challenge - Your future self will thank you for it.

Good luck with everything!!!
