Hi my name is Kaitlyn and I have some advice for you!

Make Connections!

The more ways you can connect things you're learning to things you already know, the better you'll understand and remember them.

  • Reading your knitting patterns like they're code
  • Use naming consistent naming conventions to organize your computer
  • Think of finding errors in your code like playing your favorite hidden objects game.

Try It Again

Repetition is key. Even better is doing it over in a different place or a different way. Strengthen those neural pathways by varying your practice.

Take a Break

Most importantly remember that your brain needs time to store new information and taking a break is often the best way to solve your most difficult problems.
