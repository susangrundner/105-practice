Hi, my name is Jennifer. Here's some advice for you...

Read the Literature Before You DO the Steps

Web Development is like learning a new language. Before you jump into the deep end of tech, I highly recommend to read through the lessons!

As boring as reading can get, you will not be as lost and confused when working on each exercises. Oh, and not to freak out!

The More You Understand, the Better You'll Be.

To read is to learn. To learn is to apply. I am much of a doer in order to understand the technical aspects, but I retain the information just as much when I read through Skillcrush's lessons and grasp the new vocabulary.

No one is perfect and is a master right off the bat, but you must understand what you are reading and practicing in order to save time later.

Even if you don't do well the first time, the beauty of code and science is to try again.
