Hi my name is Anna and I have some advice for you!

It won't seem like you're learning much at first

At first, it felt like I was dumping new information in my brain every day, but that nothing was sticking. I didn't think it was ever going to come together and that I'd really be able to do anything real with what I was learning!

But one day I was working on a project, and I realized that I knew what I was doing or at least knew where to get answers. I really was learning!

Just keep putting in the time every day

I've heard in many disciplines a little bit of time every day is better than a lot of time spent sporadically. I feel this is very true in coding! When I miss a day or two in my coding practice it sets me back and I need to do more review and catching up. When I do at least a little every day it feels like it keeps things fresh in my brain, and I'm able to keep making progress more quickly.

So even on the days you don't have much time, try and put in at least a few minutes!
