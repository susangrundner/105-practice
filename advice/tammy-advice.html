Hi, I'm Tammy, and here are some words of advice, from one code newbie to another...

Banish self-doubt.

Imposter syndrome happens to everyone, so believe in yourself, and if you need to doubt something, doubt your self-doubt.


Work really hard.

This seems obvious, but you get what you give, so get out of your comfort zone.


Don't be afraid to get help.

The dev communinity is very helpful, and there are many resources to find answers to anything that is stumping you.


Immerse yourself in the tech world.

Listen to podcasts, read articles, talk to like-minded people. Even if you have no idea what anything means, you will, eventually.
