Hi, my name is Misty and I have some advice for you!

Consistency is Key!

Do you find yourself studying and practicing only during weekend binge sessions? Try coding (mostly) every day!

Even if it's just for 15 minutes each day, the consistency will help you develop the habits and skills you need to meet your coding goals.

Make it Fun!

Are there other hobbies or interests you want to blend into your tech life? Incorporate them into your projects!

Use your newfound tech skills to solve problems in other areas of your life.
