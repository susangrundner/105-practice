Hi, my name is Thomas and I have some advice for you!

Just jump right in!

It can be daunting starting to learn a new skill especially if you are working full time and have been out of school for a while. That's why I recommend not to over think it and just start learning! There's no rush in learning something new and only future benefits.

It's easy to find excuses to put off studying and to have fun or just relax; but, you've got to put those feelings aside and come to realize that you are doing this for your future and that you can rest when you're done!

Take a step back and see how far you've come.

You need to put be consitently be doing you're lessons and working on the next step. That doesn't mean that you can't take a small break or reward yourself after completing a diffulcult session. I find taking a breather and looking back at what I've done can be excellent motivating to keep on chugging away.

I hope this advice will help someone along their journey and I know you have it in you. Keep doing the work and I'm sure we'll both be well on our way to becoming amazing coders in no time!
