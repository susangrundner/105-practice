Hi, my name is Magda and I have some advice for you on how to lead a healthy and happy life!

Take care of your mind and body like someone you love

  • Treat yourself with respect and don't say or think negative things about yourself
  • Find time for yourself to do what gives you peace and happiness
  • Spend time with those you love and be kind to others
  • Go out in nature often
  • Travel to new places near and far
  • Think positively - life's trials always happen for a reason
  • Find a job that will keep you interested and motivated
  • Never stop learning and researching new things
  • Exercise and stretch your body
  • Pray, meditate, journal
  • Breathe deeply and consciously
  • Drink lots of water
  • Eat whole unprocessed mainly plant-based foods
  • Establish a healthy sleep routine
  • Be mindful of and limit your exposure to EMFs
  • Cleanse and detox your body once or twice a year
  • If you feel stressed or anxious - go for a walk to clear your mind

Go with the flow and appreciate all that life brings your way
