Hi, my name is Crystal  and I have some advice for you!

Stay as consistent as you can.

Consistency matters overall. If you get off course, make a route to jump back into your studies when you can. It's important to say, "Hey, this is important to me."

Even just 5 or 10 minutes of study can help you get back to being consistent and start on a regular schedule.

Have fun with it.

I am the most inspired when I am working on something with a playful attitude. I just keep practicing projects on my own until I'm comfortable with what I've learned from them.

Creativity is the best learning partner.
