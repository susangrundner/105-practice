Hi, my name is Sam and I have
some motivation for you!

You're new at this- it's okay to make mistakes!

We all started learning how to code due to different reasons: looking for a new job after being furloughed due to COVID-19, getting a first job, trying out a new hobby because you're bored with painting this week- whatever your reason may be, don't quit now!

You've come this far, so why quit now?

You're going to make mistakes, some things are going to be more exciting than others, and other things just seem like a nucance (why do we need to use 'em' as a measurement when 'px' works just fine? LOL).

But do you remember how cool it was to finish your very first website, Jubilee Austen? And you bought a domain so you can send that link to your friends and family and say "Hey! Look what I made!" You gotta admit seeing that online for anyone to see is magical, and I don't know about you, but that's what keeps me going.

I hope you keep going too.
