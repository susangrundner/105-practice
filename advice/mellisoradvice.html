Hello skillcrusher, here is some advice...

Confusion is your friend!

Try to remain curious and stay positive. You will figure it out. The learning process involves being confused at times.

Remember, everyone starts out confused.

Suspended disbelief plays an important role.

You will understand everything more fully with time and your understanding will continue to evolve.

Enjoy the process!

Practice, practice, practice. Even when you are really confused and don't understand exactly what you are doing, just keep trying. The only way through is through.
