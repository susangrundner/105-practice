Hi, my name is Laura and I have some advice for you!

Just start.

It has taken a quarantine to force me to finally put some meaningful time into this course, but I see now just how much I could have learned even doing a couple hours of work a week.

Little by little, you will find yourself learning a brand new skill you never imagined you could know. But you have to start somewhere.

Take your time.

This journey has taken me two years... so far. I kept coming up with excuses to not continue, but they were never enough to stop me completely.

No matter how long it takes, it is worth it. You don't have to let the fear of failure take over.

Start over again if you need - or want - to.

There will always be roadblocks. I have had months between lessons, and I always came back feeling completely lost.

If you find yourself in the same boat, don't worry. It doesn't mean you shouldn't continue. It is completely normal to have to take a step back and find your footing.

I even had to re-learn how to ride a bike, and isn't there a saying about not needing to?
