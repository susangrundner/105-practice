Have faith:
Survival guide 2 to overwhelming challenges.

Be ready for an infinity of problems!

I can't even begin to count how many times I sat at my computer and thought "Gosh! where do I even begin?!" and then doubted my skills or capabilities to overcome the task, and felt the temptation to ask somebody for help. But I think one of the best tips I read was "you're not a programmer, you're a problem solver", This helped shift my perspective from "Am I good enough to do this?" to ""Let's see how we can find a solution to this problem". Perhaps coding is very much like doing sudoku or solving a crossword, or putting together a puzzle. As a problem solver, you expect problems!

All doors have a key

Following from the last point, I can't even begin to count the number of times I've thought I couldn't solve THIS problem, but the truth is that it's all in starting, and insisting, sticking at it. Everything will unfold little by little. The solution is really out there, ALWAYS!

It's inevitable

Keep pushing, the problem WILL buckle.
