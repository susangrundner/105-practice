Hi my name is Alexandra and I have some advice for you!

If you're female look at inspiring women in tech!

Women are often overlooked in specific industries, and it's important to find women to inspire you. Keep up the excellent work and the good fight and keep coding! Just because you're female doesn't mean you can't excel in this field!

Read books that inspire you

One thing that really helped me believe in myself was a book called Growth Mindset by Carol Dweck. It states that in order to excel you need what's called a "growth-mindset". This means that you need to believe that you can do something.

In otherwords, intelligence is fluid and in order to do well you need to continue to believe this. IQ isn't fixed. Just because you've tested at an IQ score of 90 doesn't mean you'll stay there forever.

In fact, I give IQ tests at work, and at least for the verbal sections, you can improve your score based on how much vocab you know! That means you can raise your score dramatially! How cool is that?

Keep fighting the good fight

Don't give up. Keep going even if it gets hard. Just because something is hard now doesn't mean that it'll be hard forever. I spent an hour fixing my code on one of the Skillcrush assignments until I got it right. Finally, after many trials I managed to succeed! Just keep going and realize that if you need help that's okay.
