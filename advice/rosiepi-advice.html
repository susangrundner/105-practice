From one newbie to another...

Practice, practice, practice!

When you do a little every day, all your new skills stay fresh in your brain and you will have to do less reviewing and get to do more practicing, which will grow your confidence. Think of it like working out, you feel stronger and more confident in your body when you stay consistent. Most of this will not come easy, but it will become more comfortable the more you practice!

Pay attention!

There is a lot to learn and a lot of different skills to acquire. Pay attention to what lights you up, or at least excites you to learn. Follow those bread crumbs and it will lead you to the speciality that will lead to the fulfilling career you aimed to arrive at when starting this journey.
