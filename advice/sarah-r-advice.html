Hi, my name is Sarah R, and I have some advice for you!

Learn from multiple sources

Let me start out by saying that Skillcrush is awesome.

Second, when you learn something new, learn it from multiple sources. While one source may seem to cover everything, it's helpful to get a perspective from another source. This will help you practice and it will solidify your understanding of the topic as a whole.
