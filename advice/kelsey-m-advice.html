Hi, my name is Kelsey and I have some advice for you!

We all have Imposter Syndrome!

Technology is constantly changing and no one is an expert in all things coding. It's impossible! We all struggle with moments of doubt, but that is where a supportive community is the best! Having a couple people who can cheer you on in your moments of doubt is essential.

No question is a dumb question and mistakes are the pathway to success! Don't fear asking a question in the developer community. You are learning and most devs are more than happy to lend a helping hand. You only get to be a beginner once, so take advantage and ask for help! It doesn't make you less of a developer, in my opinion, it makes you a better developer!

Celebrate the litte successes.

It's very easy to gloss over the little things, but don't.

There will be plenty of times you get stuck and confused. Learning to celebrate the little wins help make the hard times easier to manage.

You are a developer. You are doing this. You are CRUSHING it.
