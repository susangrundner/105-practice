Hi, my name is Mariah and I have some advice for you!

Take time every day!

When you are learning a new skill, it is important to take time every day to work on it.

There are going to be new terms and skills to learn every day, and if you let huge amounts of time pass in between practicing, you will have to re-learn those skill in order to move on.

Life gets busy, but I suggest taking at least 30 minutes a day to practice your new skill.

Keep Going!

There will be bumps and twists in the journey of learning, but it is important to not give up.

Look for the nuggets of success gold and use those as fuel to push you through the difficult times.

Use your resources! Talk to someone who knows better than you. Use the internet! Chances are, your problem has already been solved by someone and they probably posted about it online!
