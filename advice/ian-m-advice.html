Hi, my name is Ian and I have some advice for you!

Take notes

It may seem obvious, but it's very tempting to rely on the computer to store all the information for you.

Taking notes on an actual notepad allows you to reference what you may be missing or stuck on, without having to cycle through windows, looking for the answer.

Title the pages of your notes, and it will be very easy to find what you're looking for. Faster too.

Google it

Something weird happened. Oh no! There's no info on Skillcrush or in your notes. Don't worry, Google is your friend.

Most of the issues you will have have been had by other people who have written asked or written about that issue online.

What a better place to ask about your issues with the internet than the internet! As the good doctor Steve Bruhl would probably say...

“Google it, you dummy!”
