Practice Every Day!

I have found that when I make it a point to practice everyday for at least 20 minutes, it makes such a difference!

Being a mom of two boys, I have found that just taking 20 minutes a day to learn something new in coding can be so beneficial.

"Continuous effort — not strength or intelligence — is the key to unlocking our potential."

- Liane Cardes

Programming can be so intimidating to many. There are so many aspects that you learn and it is constantly changing.

The way I am looking at it is, you are creating the habits you want for your future career!
