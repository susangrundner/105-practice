Coding, A rewarding journey!

My Experience and Advice

I would be lying to you if I told you that learning to code is going to be easy! It is going to be a very difficult process. I make many mistakes every single day. However, I enjoy every moment of it. I also learned not to be scared of error messages, they are there to help you become a better developer.

Helpful Tips

  • Have confidence in your ability to learn!
  • Celebrate your small successes.
  • Programmers never stop learning.
  • Error messages are truly your best friend! Read them.
  • Ask for help if all else fails.
  • Enjoy the process.
