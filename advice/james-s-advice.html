Hi, my name is James and I have some advice for you!

Take it one day at a time!

Learning to code takes time, you are not going learn everything there is about code overnight. Focus on one lesson at a time, once you have an full understanding, move on to the next lesson.

Believe in yourself.

In order to move forward in the program you have to believe in yourself, if not, it will lead to stagnation, or worse, giving up.

So, make the choice to believe in yourself, you can do this!
