Hi, my name is Brian and I have some advice for you!

Confusion is only a state of mind!

You don't want to make any mistakes, but you cannot learn without making some!

Confusion, therefore, is a sign that you are heading in the right direction.

Fear plays an important role.

When you are learning a new technology, you have to push the envelope or you will stay in the same position. Unchanged.

This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.

When you encounter a new programming language, part of your learning process includes learning to train your eye. The way to train your eye is to read a good deal of code even when you don't totally understand what it's doing. It's uncomfortable for most Newbies.
