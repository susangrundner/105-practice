Hi, my name is Kerstin and I have some advice for you!

Believe in Yourself!

Be kind to yourself. You're learning something new. Know that there will be good days where you know, without a doubt, this is where you are meant to be and then other days where you feel like you've hit a wall and the doubt creeps in. This is where you grow. Don't give up on you!

Focus, focus, focus

You are here for a reason. Don't forget why. Brush the dust off, revamp those goals if you need to, but don't let them grow stale!
