Hi, my name is Nancy and I have some advice for you!

Take Notes! Lots of them!

I bought a set of Black and Red notebooks. I also got some funky stickers and pasted them on different notebooks so that I would know which book was which. (It also makes it fun too!)

While I'm studying if something new comes up I'll quickly write a few notes or make some drawings. I find that writing things down while I'm learning helps the ideas "stick".

I have a book for each subject I'm studying (HTML/CSS, Javascript, React, Python. I also have a notebook entitled "Passion Projects". I've made a list of apps I'd like to create. I keep ideas of things I need to learn before I can finish a project. That way I can better focus my learning and keep my goals in front of me.

"Layer" your Learning

What I mean by that is to learn from different sources. Sometimes when I'm learning a new concept I'll check out MDN or w3schools or I'll read different articles on some of the blogs that I follow. Sometimes having a concept explained different ways really helps to understand it. It also helps to see different ways to use a new tool.
