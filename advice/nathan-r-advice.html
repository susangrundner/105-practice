Hi, my name is Nate and I have some advice for you!


Be patient!

When you begin this journey you will feel overwhelmed at first.

Hang in there! It will begin to come together and make more sense. The many questions you hold in the back of your mind will be answered as you get deeper into the lessons.

Practice, practice, practice!

Like in most things, practice makes perfect.

Designate some time in your day as "playtime" to simply explore what different bits of code can do.
