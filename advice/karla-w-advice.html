Hi, my name is Karla and I have some advice for you!

Learning to code will be tough...

...but so worth it! Learning to code is like learning a new language (quite literally...). It's going to be confusing at first and it's going to take a lot of practice and perseverance. When you get stuck and start feeling frustrated, give yourself a break. When you come back, you'll be refreshed and ready to tackle the problem!

The internet is your friend

The internet is a wonderful tool and will more than likely have an answer to all your coding questions. Get in the habit of doing your own research before asking a peer, it'll keep your problem-solving skills in tip-top shape and with so many great coding resources out there, chances are that you'll find your answer way faster that way.

If you just can't wrap your head around a problem, reach out to the coding community through forums like Stack Overflow or the Skillcrush community on Slack.
