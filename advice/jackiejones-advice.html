Hi my name is Jackie and I have some advice for you!

Confusion is your friend!

This stuff is hard. If you're new to the tech industry and are making a career change from something completely different, learning to code really does feel like learning a foreign lanuage. YOU CAN DO THIS! Keep working at it, keep practicing, and don't be too hard on yourself.

Keep working at it!

It won't necessarily come easy...but it will come. I found a trick to help make some things a little less confusing. I started a binder where I would print off the provided "cheat sheets" and took some notes as I went along. It made it easier to refer back when I would start working on the projects. I also feel this is something I can refer back to in the future.
