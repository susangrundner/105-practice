Try the Pomodoro Technique!

Sometimes it can be hard to motivate yourself to keep up with your Skillcrush classes, especially when you feel like you're not making progess. This is when I like to use the Pomodoro Technique, invented by developer (hey!) Francisco Cirillo.

Set a timer for 25 minutes and work without any distractions. When time is up, take a 5 minute break. This 30 minute chunk of time is called a "Pomodoro". After 4 Pomodoros, take a longer break. It's amazing what you can accomplish when you have to work for just 25 minutes at a time!
