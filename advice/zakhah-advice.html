Know Your Why

Remember why you signed up for this course.

Was it because you wanted more income? Did you want to reinvent yourself? Did you think the tech world is awesome? Whatever the reason, stay focused on that and it will get you through the hard times.

Do Your Research

If you get stuck, there are a number of resources to help you.

Research the answer on line. Tap into the Skillcrush community. Join Facebook groups.

For every problem there is a solution. Someone is there to help you even if that someone is yourself.
