Confusion is real, but don't let it defeat nor detour you

You are learning a brand spanking new skill and like everything else it is confusing and can even be a little scary. The best part of learning programing, is the answer is out there, somewhere. Remember all of your disposable resources and when in doubt Google, Google, Google.

Stay Motivated
