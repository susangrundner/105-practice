Getting to Carnagie Hall .com

The only thing that truly makes all of the gears start to
click is practice, practice and more practice.
I try and find new ways to customize or change things
to improve them. Often, this breaks them first. I try and use
this as a chance to practice troubleshooting!

Never the less, she persisted

I need to be better at this one. Things break. Typos happen.
It is EASY to miss a missing . - look how small that is!
Take a deep breath and go over it again. You'll find the error.
Sometimes, I find it best to just walk away for a while, or sleep on it.
When I pick it up again, with a clear head, I usually find the error quickly!

Curiosity - It's not just for cats!

Try things. Look up new ways to do something. Push the idea further.
This medium is made to be tinkered with… improved upon!
Once you print a brochure, you are stuck with that color, that font, that TYPO!
That is the beauty of web design - it is iterative.
Take advantage of that and never stop improving!
