Details Even For the Detail-Oriented

Follow each lesson like it's an important recipe. So many times I would get distraught thinking I didn't understand something and then I would go back line by line through the instructions and realize I had missed something. The fix was on a silver platter, it was so basic and I was off on another planet searching complex solutions online. FACEPALM. Sometimes it is really simple and you're OVERestimating a solution.

Just Show Up!

It is really that easy!! All you have to do is try a little bit everyday.

Also - this seems the opposite - but it's also OKAY when you cannot show up. Don't be hard on yourself, just tell yourself "It's okay I didn't figure out why that background image wasn't working. It's OKAY."
